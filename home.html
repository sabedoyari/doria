NEGOCIO

Productos Alimenticios Doria is the leading Colombia manufacturer in the pasta business. During the production process (raw material handling, dough preparation, extrusion, drying, cooling, and sorting) different types of waste cutouts are generated which have an impact on operative efficiency and environmental issues.

Currently, Doria' s waste quantities have an estimated average per year from 2018 to 2020 of 487 tons on different segments, being pasta waste cutouts the majority of this proportion of about 40.72%. This is why Doria looks forward to identifying the main factors in the production of dry and wet pasta cutout waste inside the different production lines.
